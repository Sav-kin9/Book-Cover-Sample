The System

Rules before
every trade.

1
NY Session Only
All entries taken in the New York session. No London chasing, no overnight holds.
2
Confirmed Close Entry
Never enter on a zone touch. Wait for candle close with confirmation before executing.
3
Two-Tier Take Profit
TP1 secures partials at 2R. TP2 runs the trade to 4R. Single-TP exits are a protocol violation.
4
No Thursday Gold
Thursday permanently removed after a 36% win rate. The data made the decision — not me.
5
Grade Before Entry
Every setup graded A+ to F. Only A and B grades qualify for execution.
6
Loss Streak Protocol
3 consecutive losses triggers a mandatory review. No revenge trades. No exceptions.

"The market doesn't care about your confidence. It rewards your system."

Every rule was written in the language of loss — a mistake made, analyzed, and converted into a constraint that protects future capital.

Trading Psychology

The edge between
the ears.

Emotion-Neutral Execution

A trade is a business decision. Not a feeling. Win or loss, the process stays identical. The rulebook governs every click — not mood, not streak, not hope.

Mandatory Journaling

Every trade is graded, journaled, and reviewed. No journal entry — no next trade. Pattern recognition comes from data and records, not from memory and selective recall.

Weekly Reset Protocol

Weekly-cycle resets replace the dangerous "reset after one win" habit. Risk is calculated fresh at the start of each week — never mid-streak, never mid-emotion.
